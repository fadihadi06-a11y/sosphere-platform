JOURNEY 1 — Company Owner First Visit (www.sosphere.com)

Step 1: Landing page
- What does the owner see first?
- Is there a clear CTA to sign up?
- Is there a demo/trial option?
- What information is shown about the product?
- Are pricing plans visible before signup?

Step 2: Registration flow
- What fields are required?
- Is phone number mandatory? Is it verified with real OTP?
- Is business email mandatory? Is it validated as business 
  (not gmail/yahoo)?
- Is there a company name field?
- Is there a company size selector (small/medium/large)?
- What happens after registration — email verification?

Step 3: Company setup wizard
- After registration, what is the setup flow?
- Is there a step-by-step wizard?
- What are the steps? (company info, zones, employees, etc.)
- Can owner skip steps and return later?
- Is progress saved if they close and return?
- What is the minimum setup to reach the dashboard?

Step 4: Zone creation
- How does owner create zones?
- What fields are required per zone?
- Is there a map interface or just text input?
- Can zones be imported in bulk?
- Minimum/maximum zones per plan?

Step 5: Employee onboarding method
- Does the system offer:
  a) Manual entry (name, phone, email, zone, role)
  b) CSV import
  c) Invite via SMS/email
  d) All of the above?
- What is the exact CSV format expected?
  Show all column headers required.
- What validation happens on CSV import?
  What happens with invalid rows?
- Is there a CSV template available to download?

Step 6: Invitation system
- When owner invites employee, what exactly is sent?
  SMS? Email? Both?
- What does the invitation contain?
  - Download app link?
  - Company join code?
  - Admin name shown?
  - Zone assignment shown?
- What is the exact format of the invite message?
- How long is the invite link/code valid?
- Can owner resend invites? Bulk resend?
- What if employee's phone number is wrong?

Step 7: Employee receives invitation
- Employee downloads app
- Opens app — what is the first screen?
- They enter company code/link — what happens?
- Do they see: admin name? zone? company name?
- Is admin automatically set as first emergency contact?
- How does employee add other emergency contacts?
- Can employee see their zone on a map?

Step 8: Admin approves employee
- How does admin know an employee has requested to join?
- Where in dashboard does approval happen?
- What information is shown about the pending employee?
- Can admin approve multiple at once?
- What does employee see while waiting for approval?
- What happens when admin approves?
  - Employee gets notification?
  - Employee account is activated?
  - Employee appears in dashboard?

Step 9: Subscription and billing
- Is pricing based on employee count? Zone count? Both?
- What are the exact plan tiers and limits?
- How does the pricing calculator work?
- What payment methods are accepted?
- Is there a free trial? How many days? What are limits?
- What happens when trial expires?
- Can owner upgrade mid-billing cycle?
- Is there a smart pricing suggestion based on 
  company size selected during registration?
